About Sreevision

SreeVision the Magnum fest conducted every year in Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology is always commited to work for and achieve its motto is to instill technical education in the budding techies but in a fun and rich manner.
Despite being called a technical fest this fest includes many non-technical events to prove that it's definitely not restricted only to techies but also to all fun loving students to get a break from daily academics.

SREEVISION was initiated in year 2009. It involves events like Technical, Cultural, Sports, Funden and Mirth.
Mirth is the cult Night which is an inter-college cultural pavement that provides the students enormous enthusiasm and refreshment as a part of overall personality development. The talent displayed so far, promises not just a change in the future, but the future itself. Every year of Sreevision has transcended its former self and this tradition remains to continue into the future..
